A decentralized BCH/USD price feed on Bitcoin Cash chipnet. Seven notaries co-witness prices from thirteen exchanges (9 USD-quoted, 2 USDC, 2 USDT); any publisher relays them to a smart contract that commits the median on chain.

The notaries are the only off-chain trust assumption — corrupting a majority of them (≥ 4 of 7) is the only attack the design admits; everything else is rejected by script.

Read the price. On-chain: spend a Ticker NFT as an input, decode its 17-byte commit. Off-chain: GET /api/v1/price. Integration guide, signature inventory, and operator playbook in the docs.
