A decentralized BCH/USD price feed on Bitcoin Cash chipnet. A smart contract takes the median of ≥ 7 of 13 publisher attestations and commits it on chain. Every 60s.

The trust root is the 7-of-13 publisher quorum: corrupting fewer than 7 publishers can't move the median; the covenant absorbs ≤ 6 outliers without bias. No admin keys, no upgrade path.

Read the price on chain. Spend a Ticker NFT as an input in your transaction and decode its 17-byte commit. Full integration guide, signature inventory, and operator playbook in the docs.

Reading the price

There is one path: on chain. Spend any of the live Ticker NFTs (minted by the Oracle covenant, anyone-can-spend, self-replicating via Ticker.cash) as an input in your transaction. Your tx becomes a chain-descendant of the Oracle.update that minted that Ticker — BCH consensus refuses to confirm it in any history that excludes the update.

Decode tx.inputs[N].nftCommitment as the 17-byte commit:

0x80 | seq(4) | lastTs(4) | medianPrice(8)

Little-endian throughout. medianPrice / 10⁸ = USD. Pin the Oracle category (see the Contracts sidebar) as a covenant arg so an attacker can't feed you a Ticker from a different oracle.

Full guide — reference covenant, freshness gates, verifying the median — in docs §06 Integrate.
