A decentralized BCH/USD price feed on Bitcoin Cash chipnet. A smart contract takes the median of ≥ 7 of 13 publisher attestations and commits it on chain. Every 60 s.

The trust root is the 7-of-13 publisher quorum: corrupting fewer than 7 publishers can't move the median; the covenant absorbs ≤ 6 outliers without bias. No admin keys, no upgrade path.
