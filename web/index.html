A decentralized BCH/USD price feed on Bitcoin Cash chipnet. A smart contract takes the median of ≥ 7 of 13 publisher attestations and commits it on chain. Every 60s.

The trust root is the 7-of-13 publisher quorum: corrupting fewer than 7 publishers can't move the median; the covenant absorbs ≤ 6 outliers without bias. No admin keys, no upgrade path, no governance signers — the rules are in Oracle.cash + PublisherSlot.cash, deployed once, immutable.

Read the price. On-chain: spend a Ticker NFT as an input, decode its 17-byte commit. Off-chain: GET /api/v1/price. Full integration guide, signature inventory, and operator playbook in the docs · live federation state at stats.
